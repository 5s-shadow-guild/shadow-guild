About Shadow

•SHADOW• is a level 7 guild that was founded on July 5, 2010 by •ice17•, who is now known as LadyAice. This guild originated from Highstreet 5 PH. Currently, the guild is composed of 90% Filipinos and 10% from other countries.

The mission of this guild is to foster a supportive and inclusive community where members can collaborate, share their gaming experiences, and achieve collective goals while having fun and building lasting friendships.
